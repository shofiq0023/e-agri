about our website

Now a day, E-commerce is the most visible business use of the World Wide Web. The initial purpose of an e-commerce site is to sell the goods or products online. Our website deals with developing a web based market place where daily necessary items can be bought and sold also delivered home within a short amount of time at lower cost. It offers a user to be a product seller or buyer. It provides user the item category lists and also the seller’s profile in rural & urban areas. In order to develop an e-commerce site the number of technology must be studied and understood.
This system include multi-tiered architecture, server and client side scripting techniques. This is a website where buyer and seller can interact with each other in order to buy and sell products through a platform and also to know the technology uses to develop such an application.

Bangladesh is an agriculture dependent country. Agriculture is the largest employment in Bangladesh. Online agriculture is becoming a suitable way to make all your purchases crops, whether you’re at home or anywhere. Taking to online agriculture to search Crops or service, compare prices & to shop by best quality.
We all want to get products within low cost & best quality. This web based application will be able to fulfill your expectation. Users are able to see the product cost and also the seller profile and can also interact with them through number.

idea & concept

E-Agriculture.com is an online Marketplace where anyone can find better and trustworthy products within Bangladesh. It is the first open agricultural product platform in Bangladesh. Marketers, stockists, marketers and institutional consumers will get the opportunity to verify prices and quality across the country and have direct commercial communication on the same platform, starting from being a farmer directly through E-Agriculture nationwide network.
